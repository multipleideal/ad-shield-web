This Privacy Policy describes how Multiple Ideal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, Image Resizer (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Image Processing & Data Storage

We respect your privacy. The core functionality of the App (resizing images by Storage, Pixel, or Ratio) is performed locally on your device.

  • No Cloud Uploads: We do not upload your original or resized photos to any cloud server. All image processing happens directly on your phone's processor.
  • Gallery Access: The App requires access to your device's storage solely to allow you to select images for processing and to save the resized results back to your Gallery.

2. Permissions We Request

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: This is used to display your images so you can choose which ones to resize.
  • INTERNET: This is used solely by Google AdMob to display advertisements and to check network status.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. This allows us to offer the App for free.

Google AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads or to measure ad performance. This data may include:

  • Device Identifiers (such as Advertising ID)

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
